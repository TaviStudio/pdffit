Overview

PDF Fit makes it easy to compress PDFs to fit your selected file size while preserving the best achievable document quality.

Simply import or create a PDF, scan a paper document, or add photos, choose your target file size, and let PDF Fit do the rest.

Intelligent Compression automatically analyzes each page to optimize compression for text, mixed content, and photos, delivering the best possible balance between quality and file size.

Professional Scan automatically detects document boundaries, corrects perspective, optimizes readability, and creates each PDF page in the detected format while preserving content near the edges.

Import photos and create clean, professional PDFs with automatic page sizing, preserved proportions, and document-aware optimization.

Whether you need to reduce a PDF for online submission, email attachments, or file size limits, PDF Fit makes PDF compression fast, simple, and reliable.

Built-in file size presets for email attachments, messaging apps, visa applications, job applications, and more make it easy to choose the right file size in seconds.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

PDF Fit is designed to process documents privately on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how PDF Fit handles information when you use the app.

Data Collection

PDF Fit does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not require an account and does not collect names, email addresses, contact information, device identifiers, location data, advertising data, tracking information, or the contents of your documents and photos.

Document Processing

PDF files, scanned documents, and photos selected in PDF Fit are processed entirely on your device.

Your documents and images are not uploaded to PDF Fit servers or to any external server operated by the developer.

PDF Fit does not retain copies of your imported PDFs, scans, photos, or exported documents on developer-controlled servers.

Camera Access

PDF Fit may request access to your device camera when you choose to scan a paper document.

Camera access is used only to capture the documents you choose to scan. Captured pages are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to the developer.

Photo Library Access

PDF Fit may request access to photos when you choose to create a PDF from images.

Photo access is used only for the images you select. PDF Fit does not upload your photos to developer-controlled servers.

Files Access

PDF Fit accesses a PDF or storage location only when you select it through Apple’s document picker, Files interface, or Share Sheet.

The app uses the selected file only to perform the action requested by you, such as previewing, compressing, renaming, saving, or sharing the PDF.

Saved and Shared Files

Files are saved only when you choose to save or export them through Apple’s Files interface or another available system option.

When you share a document using the iOS Share Sheet, the selected third-party app or service may process the file according to its own privacy policy. PDF Fit does not control the privacy practices of third-party apps or services selected by you.

In-App Purchases

Lifetime Pro purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

PDF Fit does not receive or store your payment card details. Apple may provide the app with purchase status information required to unlock or restore Lifetime Pro.

App Store purchases are processed according to Apple’s applicable terms and privacy policies.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

PDF Fit does not use third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, profiling tools, or tracking technologies.

The app does not track you across apps or websites and does not use your information for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

PDF Fit does not upload documents to third-party cloud processing or artificial intelligence services.

System features provided by Apple, including the camera, Photos picker, Files picker, Share Sheet, and App Store purchase system, may operate under Apple’s applicable terms and privacy policies.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because PDF Fit does not collect or store personal data on developer-controlled servers, there is no personal account data for the developer to retain or delete.

You remain in control of the PDFs, photos, scans, and exported files stored on your device and may delete them at any time using the relevant Apple apps or storage locations.

Security

On-device processing is used to reduce the exposure of your documents to external systems. You are responsible for securing your device, backups, shared files, and selected storage destinations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if PDF Fit’s functionality or data-handling practices change. Any updated policy will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

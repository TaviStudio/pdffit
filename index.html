PDF Fit

Compress PDFs to fit your selected file size.

Overview

PDF Fit makes it easy to compress PDFs to fit your selected file size while preserving the best achievable document quality.

Simply import or create a PDF, scan a paper document, or add photos, choose your target file size, and let PDF Fit do the rest.

Intelligent Compression automatically analyzes each page to optimize compression for text, mixed content, and photos, delivering the best achievable balance between quality and file size.

Professional Scan automatically detects document boundaries, corrects perspective, optimizes readability, and creates each PDF page in the detected format while preserving content near the edges.

Import photos and create clean, professional PDFs with automatic page sizing, preserved proportions, and document-aware optimization.

Whether you need to reduce a PDF for online submission, email attachments, or file size limits, PDF Fit makes PDF compression fast, simple, and reliable.

Built-in file size presets for email attachments, messaging apps, visa applications, job applications, and more make it easy to choose the right file size in seconds.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

PDF Fit is designed to process documents privately on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how PDF Fit handles information when you use the app.

Data Collection

PDF Fit does not collect or transmit your personal data or document contents to the developer through the app, and it does not sell or share that information or store it on developer-controlled servers.

The app does not require an account and does not collect names, email addresses, contact information, device identifiers, location data, advertising data, tracking information, or the contents of your documents and photos through the app.

On-Device Processing and Local Storage

PDF files, scanned documents, and photos selected in PDF Fit are processed entirely on your device.

To perform the actions you request and recover work after an interruption, PDF Fit may temporarily store working copies and a pending compression result in the app’s private local storage. These files remain on your device and are removed when the related work is cleared or replaced.

PDF Fit stores limited app state locally on your device, such as your free export count and Lifetime Pro entitlement status. This information is used only to provide the app’s export allowance and purchase features and is not sent to the developer.

Your documents and images are not uploaded to PDF Fit servers or to any external server operated by the developer.

Camera Access

PDF Fit requests access to your device camera only when you choose to scan a paper document.

Camera access is used only to capture the documents you choose to scan. Captured pages are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to the developer.

Photo Selection

PDF Fit uses Apple’s Photos picker to access only the images you explicitly select when you choose to create a PDF from photos. The app does not request unrestricted access to your photo library.

Selected images are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to developer-controlled servers.

Files Access

PDF Fit accesses a PDF only when you select it through Apple’s document picker. The selected file is used only to perform the action you request, such as previewing, compressing, or renaming the PDF.

When you choose to save a PDF, Apple’s Files interface lets you select the storage location. PDF Fit does not access other files or folders that you have not selected.

Saved and Shared Files

Files are saved or exported only when you choose to do so. Exported files remain in the destination you select until you delete them.

When you share a document using the iOS Share Sheet, the third-party app or service you select may process the file according to its own privacy policy. PDF Fit does not control the privacy practices of third-party apps or services selected by you.

In-App Purchases

Lifetime Pro purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

PDF Fit does not receive or store your payment card details. Apple may provide the app with purchase status information required to unlock or restore Lifetime Pro.

App Store purchases are processed according to Apple’s applicable terms and privacy policies.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

PDF Fit does not use third-party analytics, advertising, cross-app tracking, profiling tools, or tracking technologies.

The app does not track you across apps or websites and does not use your information for advertising purposes.

Support Communications

If you voluntarily contact the developer by email, the developer receives the email address, message content, and any information you choose to include. This information is used only to respond to your request, provide support, and maintain necessary support records. It is not sold or used for advertising or tracking.

Third-Party Services

PDF Fit does not upload documents to third-party cloud processing or artificial intelligence services.

System features provided by Apple, including the camera, Photos picker, document picker, Files interface, Share Sheet, and App Store purchase system, may operate under Apple’s applicable terms and privacy policies.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because PDF Fit does not collect or store personal data on developer-controlled servers, there is no personal account data for the developer to retain or delete.

You remain in control of the PDFs, photos, scans, and exported files stored on your device and may delete them at any time using PDF Fit or the relevant Apple apps and storage locations. Uninstalling PDF Fit removes data stored in the app’s private local storage, subject to Apple’s device backup and restoration behavior.

Security

On-device processing reduces the exposure of your documents to external systems. You are responsible for securing your device, backups, shared files, and selected storage destinations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app’s features or privacy practices change. Any update will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
